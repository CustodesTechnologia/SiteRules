{\noindent{\textbf{Mission Statement}}\\
The BnC exists to help the members of a global hobby 
community to better understand, engage in, and enjoy all aspects of the 
Warhammer 40,000 hobby through constructive discussion and the sharing 
of hobby-related content.\\
{\noindent{\textbf{ BnC Reserves the Right to:}}\\
\hspace*{4mm} 1. Delete or modify any Member content.\\
\hspace*{4mm} 2. Disallow or modify Member account names.\\
\hspace*{4mm} 3. Change the Rules at any time for any reason.\\

{\noindent{\textbf{The Rules}}\\
1. Content shall be in English. No chat/``leet'' speak.\\
2. Members shall be permitted to have only one account.\\
3. Member content shall {\textbf{not}} have\\
\hspace*{4mm} 3-1. Disrespectful behavior (trolling, flaming, derogatory terms)\\
\hspace*{4mm} 3-2. Intellectual property violations\\
\hspace*{4mm} 3-3. ``spam''\\
\hspace*{4mm} 3-4. Software cracks\\
\hspace*{4mm} 3-5. Swearing/profanity/obscenity/pornography\\
\hspace*{4mm} 3-6. No off topic discussions}\\
\hspace*{4mm} 3-7. Threadomancy/necro-posting\\
\hspace*{4mm} 3-8. Offers to sell or trade\\
\hspace*{4mm} 3-9. Advertising unless expressly granted permission\\
